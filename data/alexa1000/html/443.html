Advertising Solutions Overview

Delivering to your audience

We offer advanced targeting by GEO location, sites, categories, devices (web and/or mobile) and keywords to reach your audience. AdsTerra's multiple ad formats are tailored to your product's requirements (see below).

Real-time data delivery

All AdsTerra advertisers are provided with an opportunity to track ad campaign performance in real-time. The analytics data are updated every minute, which enables to react on сhanges right away.

Expert service

Our support staff is available to help you at anytime. You will receive a personal account manager who will quickly set up your advertising campaigns. We accept many payment methods and sometimes we don't require a prepay for a test.

Available Targeting

  • Geo Targeting Choose from over 40 countries to give your business a global position.
  • Device Targeting Target your campaigns to specific devices, such as Mobile Phones, Laptops/Desktops, and Tablets.
  • Language Targeting Our Advertisers can target their ads to populations of different dialects to cover a more global reach.
  • OS Targeting OS Targeting allows you to target to audiences that use a specific Operating System. Systems that we can target include: Windows, Mac OS, and Linux
  • Keyword Targeting Choose highly relevant keywords; we will match and run your ads on these keywords.
  • Time Targeting Choose the exact time you want to launch your campaign to target your audience for optimal.
