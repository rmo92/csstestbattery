      Share

      Rent it through your Sky±HD box

      Never Miss

      Matthew McConaughey continues his remarkable transition from bare torsoed rom-com star to multi-faceted indie player with a richly detailed portrayal of AIDS victim Ron Woodroof. A bigoted Texan electrician, he is forced to overcome his prejudices when he's infected with HIV by a drug-addicted hooker and realises that he and other sufferers are being denied effective treatment. Skirting the law and the system, he set ups a distribution network - the Dallas Buyers Club - for alternative therapies. Sober and poignant, it's an inspiring story sublimely served by McConaughey and director Jean-Marc Vallée. Director: Jean-Marc Vallée Starring: Matthew McConaughey, Jared Leto, Jennifer Garner, Steve Zahn, Denis O'Hare (2013) (117 mins) (15)

      Chris Pine follows Harrison Ford, Ben Affleck and Alec Baldwin as the fourth incarnation of Tom Clancy's super-patriot Jack Ryan. In this back-story outing - not based on a Clancy novel - Ryan is a young marine recruited as a covert CIA analyst by Kevin Costner's veteran spook. He soon finds himself in the thick of it when he uncovers a Russian plot to cripple the US economy with a terrorist attack. But how to save the world without his girlfriend (Keira Knightley) knowing? Kenneth Branagh directs the action with the same ruthless efficiency he brings to the role of the Russian villain.
