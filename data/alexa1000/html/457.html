Why Publishers Love Taboola

  • Generate Revenue

    Monetize your site traffic with a superior RPM by inviting your users to discover high-quality sponsored articles and videos from Taboola's marketplace of publishers and brands content.

  • Enable Re-Circulation

    Eliminate "dead-ends" on your desktop and mobile sites by surfacing personalized content people may like, on every page.

  • Build a Video Business with Taboola

    Taboola started in video so we know how hard it is. Educate and direct your users to high CPM video pages by introducing personalized video recommendations on articles, homepage, video page and more --  in that moment when users are about to make a decision what to consume next.

  • Control. Transparency. Simplicity.

    We provide our publishers with advanced analytics, and full editorial controls to configure the content discovery experience on their site. As an example, what content to promote, what to filter, how “fresh” should the content be, and more. Think of Taboola as your very own “Content Operating System”.

  • Because Publishers Are Users Too 

    User experience was and always will be at the heart of what we do. By striving to constantly create the ultimate content-discovery experience, publishers appreciate our service just as much as our users.
